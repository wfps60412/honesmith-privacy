# Honesmith Optics 隱私權政策 / Privacy Policy

**最後更新 / Last updated: 2026 年 6 月 25 日**

**版本標記 / Revision: `HO-20260625-DIAGNOSTICS-EMAIL-V2`**

## 摘要

Honesmith Optics 的一般拍攝、錄影、對焦、曝光、外接鏡 Profile、Recipe 與顯示偏好，預設都在你的裝置上運作。App 不要求帳號，不使用分析、廣告或追蹤 SDK，也不會自動把照片、影片、設定或診斷資料上傳給本 App 的開發者或第三方。

唯一可能離開裝置的情況，是你主動使用 iOS 系統分享功能，或在 App 內選擇「寄送診斷給我們」後，於 iOS 原生 Mail 撰寫畫面中自行按下「傳送」。

## 權限與用途

- **相機**：用於即時預覽、拍照、錄影、手動對焦與曝光控制。
- **麥克風**：只在你開始錄影時用於收音。
- **照片圖庫（僅新增）**：用於把你拍攝的照片與影片儲存到你的照片圖庫。App 不讀取、列舉或分析你既有的照片圖庫。

你可隨時在 iOS「設定」調整或撤回權限。若未授權「新增照片」，App 會在拍攝前提示你前往設定，不會開始一個已知無法儲存的拍攝流程。

## 本機資料

下列資料僅保留在你的裝置上，除非你自行匯出或寄送：

- 外接鏡 Profile、Recipe、顯示偏好與其他 App 設定
- 拍攝流程產生的本機中繼資料與使用者可選擇匯出的診斷 JSON
- 你拍攝後由 iOS 儲存到「照片」App 的照片與影片

刪除 App 會移除 App 儲存的本機資料。已儲存到「照片」App 的照片與影片仍由「照片」App 管理，請在「照片」App 中刪除。

## 診斷報告與你主動寄送的 Email

診斷報告只在你主動建立時產生。你可以使用 iOS 系統分享方式自行決定分享管道，或選擇「寄送診斷給我們」。

選擇寄送時，App 會先向你說明診斷資料內容，然後開啟 iOS 原生 Mail 撰寫畫面，並附上診斷 JSON。只有你在 Mail 中自行按下「傳送」後，資料才會寄往 [honesmithstudio@gmail.com](mailto:honesmithstudio@gmail.com)。若你關閉或取消 Mail，資料不會離開裝置。App 不會背景上傳，也不會在 Mail 不可用時改用其他管道自動寄送。

你可在 Email 內自行填寫問題描述。若你送出 Email，本 App 的開發者可能收到：

- 你的寄件者 Email 位址與你自行輸入的文字
- 診斷 JSON，可能包含 App 與 iOS 版本、裝置型號、實體鏡頭類型、近期曝光／對焦／白平衡數值，以及近期事件或操作紀錄

診斷 JSON 不包含：

- 照片或影片內容
- 位置資訊
- 帳號、登入資料或聯絡人
- 裝置唯一識別碼
- IP 位址或 Wi‑Fi／SSID 名稱
- Recipe 或外接鏡 Profile 的自訂名稱

Email 服務供應商會依其自身隱私政策處理寄送與傳輸。本 App 的開發者不會將診斷 Email 用於廣告、行銷、追蹤或出售資料；其用途僅限於回覆支援請求、調查錯誤與改善 App。

## Recipe 與外接鏡 Profile

Recipe 用來保留拍攝模式與設定，不綁定實體鏡頭，也不會在套用時自動切換外接鏡 Profile。外接鏡 Profile 是唯一綁定實體鏡頭的項目，並保存該外接鏡的預設 EV。

## 保留與刪除支援 Email

若你主動寄送診斷 Email，開發者只會在處理支援請求、調查問題與必要追蹤所需的合理期間內保留該 Email 與附件。你可使用同一個寄件者 Email 聯絡 [honesmithstudio@gmail.com](mailto:honesmithstudio@gmail.com)，要求查詢或刪除仍由開發者保留的支援往來資料；法律、資安或防止濫用所必須保留的資料不在此限。

## 不追蹤、不出售、不作廣告

Honesmith Optics 不使用廣告 SDK、分析 SDK 或跨 App／跨網站追蹤技術；不出售個人資料，也不將資料提供給資料經紀商。

## 政策更新與聯絡方式

當 App 的資料處理方式改變時，我們會更新本頁的日期與內容。

隱私相關問題請聯絡：[honesmithstudio@gmail.com](mailto:honesmithstudio@gmail.com)

# Privacy Policy (English)

**Last updated: June 25, 2026**

**Revision: `HO-20260625-DIAGNOSTICS-EMAIL-V2`**

## Summary

Honesmith Optics performs ordinary photography, video recording, focusing, exposure control, external-lens Profiles, Recipes, and display preferences on your device by default. The app does not require an account, and it does not use analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. It does not automatically upload photos, videos, settings, or diagnostic data to the app's developer or to third parties.

Data may leave the device only when you voluntarily use an iOS system sharing option, or when you choose “Send Diagnostics to Us” and personally press **Send** in the native iOS Mail compose screen.

## Permissions and their use

- **Camera:** live preview, photography, video recording, manual focus, and exposure control.
- **Microphone:** audio recording only while you actively record video.
- **Photo Library (add-only):** saving photos and videos you capture to your library. The app does not read, enumerate, or analyze your existing photo library.

You can change or revoke permissions in iOS Settings at any time. If add-only Photos permission is unavailable, the app directs you to Settings before it begins a capture that it knows cannot be saved.

## On-device data

The following stays on your device unless you voluntarily export or send it:

- External-lens Profiles, Recipes, display preferences, and other app settings
- Local capture metadata and diagnostic JSON that you may choose to export
- Photos and videos saved by iOS to the Photos app after capture

Deleting the app removes app-local data. Photos and videos already saved to the Photos app remain managed by the Photos app and must be deleted there.

## Diagnostic reports and email you choose to send

A diagnostic report is created only when you request it. You may choose an iOS system sharing destination, or select “Send Diagnostics to Us.”

When you choose email, the app explains the report content and opens the native iOS Mail compose interface with the diagnostic JSON attached. The report is sent to [honesmithstudio@gmail.com](mailto:honesmithstudio@gmail.com) only after you personally press Send in Mail. If you cancel or close Mail, the report does not leave your device. The app does not upload diagnostics in the background and does not automatically substitute another delivery method when Mail is unavailable.

You may add a problem description to the email. If you send it, the app's developer may receive:

- Your sender email address and any text you voluntarily write
- Diagnostic JSON that may include app and iOS versions, device model, physical camera type, recent exposure/focus/white-balance values, and recent event or interaction records

The diagnostic JSON does not include:

- Photo or video content
- Location information
- Account credentials or contacts
- A unique device identifier
- IP address or Wi‑Fi/SSID name
- Custom Recipe or external-lens Profile names

Your email service provider handles transmission under its own privacy practices. The app's developer does not use diagnostic email for advertising, marketing, tracking, or sale of personal data. It is used only to respond to support requests, investigate defects, and improve the app.

## Recipes and external-lens Profiles

A Recipe saves shooting modes and settings. It does not bind to a physical camera and does not automatically switch an external-lens Profile when applied. An external-lens Profile is the only item that binds to a physical camera, and it stores that lens’s default EV.

## Retention and deletion of support email

If you voluntarily send a diagnostic email, the developer retains the email and attachment only for a reasonable period needed to address the support request, investigate the issue, and complete necessary follow-up. You may contact [honesmithstudio@gmail.com](mailto:honesmithstudio@gmail.com) from the same sender address to request access to or deletion of support correspondence the developer still retains, subject to data that must be retained for legal, security, or abuse-prevention reasons.

## No tracking, sale, or advertising

Honesmith Optics does not use advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, or cross-app/cross-site tracking. We do not sell personal data or provide it to data brokers.

## Policy updates and contact

If our data practices change, we will update this page and its date.

For privacy questions, contact: [honesmithstudio@gmail.com](mailto:honesmithstudio@gmail.com)
